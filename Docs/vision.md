# Vision

## Mission
Create a global collaboration ecosystem for developers, researchers, and builders to work together on real-world projects.

## Problem
Most Discord communities are passive and noisy. People consume content but rarely collaborate consistently or ship meaningful projects.

## Goal
Build an ecosystem where:
- developers find teammates
- researchers collaborate globally
- hackathon teams form easily
- projects are executed consistently
- AI assists workflows without replacing human interaction

## Target Users
- Developers
- AI/ML Engineers
- Open-source contributors
- Hackathon participants
- Startup builders
- Researchers

## Core Principles
- High signal over high member count
- Execution over passive discussion
- Collaboration over content consumption
- Public progress and accountability
- AI-assisted productivity

## Long-Term Vision
Evolve from a Discord-based system into a standalone global collaboration platform.
